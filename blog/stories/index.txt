Eyegrade
------------------------------------------------------

.. class:: lead

Grading multiple choice exams with a webcam

Eyegrade uses a webcam to grade MCQ (multiple choice question) exams.
Needing just a cheap low-end webcam,
it aims to be a low-cost and portable solution available to everyone,
on the contrary to other solutions based on scanners.
